Sirui Anamorphic Desqueeze Tool

Sirui anamorphic lenses come in different squeeze factors (1.33×, 1.5×, 1.8×, 2×). Use our free Web Studio or apps to de-squeeze Sirui footage: pick the matching preset, preview the correct aspect ratio, and export MP4, JPG or PNG. Available in the browser, on iPhone, Android, and Windows.

Which preset for my Sirui lens?

Check your lens specs: most Sirui anamorphics are 1.33× or 1.5×; some are 1.8× or 2×. In the Web Studio or app, select the matching preset (1.33×, 1.5×, 1.8×, or 2×). If your factor is between presets, use Custom and enter the value. Preview updates in real time.

Quick steps

  1. Open the Web Studio or the iOS/Android app.
  2. Upload your Sirui anamorphic clip(s).
  3. Choose the correct squeeze factor (1.33×–2× or custom).
  4. Export corrected MP4 or stills. Batch up to 10 files in the browser.
