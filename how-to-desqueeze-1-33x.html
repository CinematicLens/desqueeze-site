How to De-Squeeze 1.33× Anamorphic Video

1.33× anamorphic lenses are commonly used on smartphones and mirrorless cameras. They squeeze the image horizontally and must be corrected during post-processing.

Steps

  1. Open Web Desqueeze Studio
  2. Upload your anamorphic photo or video
  3. Select 1.33× de-squeeze
  4. Preview the corrected aspect ratio
  5. Export MP4, JPG, or PNG

Why 1.33× Matters

Incorrect de-squeeze results in stretched faces and distorted geometry. Correct 1.33× processing restores the intended cinematic framing.
