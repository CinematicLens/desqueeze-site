How it works

Anamorphic lenses squeeze the image horizontally; we restore the correct aspect ratio (e.g. 2.39:1). Choose your factor (1.33×–2× or custom), then export. Use the browser for quick batches, or our iOS, Android, or Windows apps for larger projects.
