What is anamorphic de-squeeze and why fix distortion?
Anamorphic lenses squeeze the image horizontally to capture a wider field of view on a normal sensor. This look is popular with professional filmmakers and mobile anamorphic lenses (Moment, Sirui, Moondog Labs). Without de-squeezing, footage looks tall and skinny. This online anamorphic de-squeeze tool fixes the distortion, stretching footage back to the intended cinematic aspect ratio (e.g., 2.39:1).
