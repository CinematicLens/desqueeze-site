2.39 : 1 export

After de-squeeze, anamorphic material belongs in CinemaScope framing (commonly 2.39:1 or 2.35:1). Upload squeezed sources, set the lens factor, confirm framing in preview, export MP4 or stills — first in the browser, heavier work on iOS or desktop apps when needed.

Pipeline in the browser

  1. Open Web Studio — no install.
  2. Upload anamorphic video or stills.
  3. Pick de-squeeze factor; verify 2.39:1 composition in preview.
  4. Export MP4 or image sequence; batch up to ten clips in one pass.

Editorial note

This path is ideal for quick client reviews, reframing checks, and lightweight handoffs. For finishing with full color pipeline, round-trip through your NLE or grading suite — our exports are meant to be correct geometry first.
