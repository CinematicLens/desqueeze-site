Cinemascope 2.39:1 Export Online

De-squeezed anamorphic footage naturally fits a Cinemascope 2.39:1 (or 2.35:1) aspect ratio. Use our free Web Studio to upload squeezed clips, choose your lens factor (1.33×–2× or custom), and export corrected MP4 or JPG frames at the right ratio — all in the browser. Also on iOS, Android, and Windows.

How to get 2.39:1 from anamorphic footage online

  1. Go to the Web Studio (no install).
  2. Upload your anamorphic video or photo.
  3. Select the correct de-squeeze factor (1.33×, 1.5×, 1.8×, or 2×). The preview shows the final 2.39:1 framing.
  4. Export MP4 for video or JPG/PNG for stills. Batch up to 10 files.
