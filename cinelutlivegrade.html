CineLutLiveGrade

Live LUT & CDL video grading on Mac — for cinematographers, DITs, and colorists. On-set grading and recording with scopes, ProRes output, and dailies export.

Get on Mac App Store

Who it’s for

Cinematographers, DOPs, DITs, colorists, and film crews who need to see the final look while the camera is rolling. Built for professional productions and independent filmmakers everywhere. Connect your SDI or HDMI camera through a capture card, grade in real time with LUTs and CDL-style controls, and record graded ProRes clips ready for editorial and dailies.

What you get

  • Live grading — 3D LUTs and ASC-CDL-style primaries (slope, offset, power, saturation). Waveform, parade, histogram, vectorscope on every frame.
  • Recording — Graded ProRes with optional burn-in (timecode, reel/scene/take, slate).
  • Dailies & handoff — Export graded ProRes, CDL, AMF, LUT files. EDL + CDL packages for Silverstack, DaVinci Resolve, and other post tools.
  • Shot library — Save stills and looks per scene. Import/export CDL and LUT. Presets and consistent looks across takes.

Why on-set matters

Unlike offline grading, CineLutLiveGrade is built for live work. You see what the camera sees, with your look applied. What you record is what the editor sees. Looks stay consistent across takes and the pipeline from set to dailies stays predictable.
