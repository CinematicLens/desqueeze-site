Anamorphic De-Squeeze for iPhone & iPad

Our iOS app (Anamorphic Desqueezer) is built for filmmakers: live anamorphic monitor and post desqueeze in one app. Supports 1.33×, 1.55×, 1.8×, and 2× (plus custom ratio). Export ProRes, HEVC 10-bit, or FCPXML for Final Cut Pro. Zebras, waveform, false color, focus peaking, and true 2.39:1 CinemaScope export. Works with Moment, Sirui, Moondog Labs, and cinema glass.

Why use the iOS app?

  • Live desqueeze monitor and post in one app.
  • ProRes, 10-bit, and FCPXML export.
  • Zebras, waveform, false color, focus peaking.
  • Batch desqueeze, RAW (DNG) support, 2.39:1 export.

Download on the App Store (iPhone & iPad). Also available: free Web Studio in your browser — upload up to 10 files, no install.

FAQ

Does it work with Moment anamorphic? Yes. Use the 1.33× preset for most Moment anamorphic lenses; the app supports 1.33×, 1.5×, 1.8× and 2×.

Can I preview 2.39:1 on set? Yes. The iOS app (and our Android apps) let you monitor de-squeezed framing in real time so you can check composition before wrapping.

Can I batch export multiple clips? Yes. Batch multiple clips in the app or in the Web Studio (up to 10 files in the browser).

Do I need a computer? No. You can de-squeeze and export entirely on iPhone or Android; use the Web Studio when you’re at a desktop for quick previews.
