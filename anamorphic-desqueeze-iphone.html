Anamorphic De-Squeeze for iPhone & iPad

Our iOS app (Anamorphic Desqueezer) gives you ratio sliders, CinemaScope presets, and batch workflows built for iPhone and iPad — on-set monitoring and post-production in one app. Supports 1.33×, 1.5×, 1.8×, and 2× anamorphic lenses (Moment, Sirui, Moondog Labs, and cinema glass).

Why use the iOS app?

  • Ratio slider and CinemaScope options for precise correction.
  • Batch process multiple clips; export video and stills.
  • On-set monitoring and director previews at 2.39:1.
  • No computer needed — fix footage on device.

Download on the App Store (iPhone & iPad). Also available: free Web Studio in your browser — upload up to 10 files, no install.

Our products

DeSqueeze Studio is available on multiple platforms with the same correction quality:

  • Web Studio — Free in-browser de-squeeze. Upload up to 10 files, choose 1.33×–2× or custom, export MP4/JPG/PNG. Launch Web Studio
  • iOS (Anamorphic Desqueezer) — Ratio sliders, CinemaScope, batch. On-set and post for iPhone & iPad. App Store
  • Android — DeSqueeze Studio — Anamorphic de-squeeze with batch and export. Google Play
  • Android — Cinematic Lens DeSqueeze — Live and post de-squeeze on Android. Google Play
