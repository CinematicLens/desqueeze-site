Photo Editing (Coming soon)

Stills are part of every production: reference frames, continuity, lighting notes, marketing selects, and quick approvals. We’re building a photo editing tool designed for filmmaker workflows so you can move faster from set to delivery.

What it will focus on

  • Filmmaker-friendly edits — quick, repeatable looks for production stills and references.
  • Fast exports — share-ready outputs for teams and clients.
  • Ecosystem fit — built to sit alongside Anamorphic Flow, LiveGrade DIT dailies, and delivery utilities.

Problems we want to solve

Productions generate thousands of stills: on-set references, lighting notes, continuity, location scouts, marketing selects, and frame grabs from dailies. Generic photo apps are not tuned for how crews name, share, and hand off those images. We want a focused tool that respects production tempo: fast culling, consistent looks, and exports that match what editorial and marketing already expect.

What you can use today

Photo Editing is not shipping yet. In the meantime, the rest of the ecosystem already covers video, anamorphic correction, grading, compression, and organization:

  • Anamorphic & framing — Anamorphic Flow on the hub, Web Studio how-to, and the iOS app.
  • Live on-set grading — CineLut Live Grade for Mac (LUT/CDL, scopes, ProRes).
  • Delivery & conversion — MediaUtility and related utilities from the Utilities section on the hub.
  • Project paperwork — Document Management System for organizing production documents on Mac.
  • Step-by-step help — Guides for choosing the right app per workflow.

Questions we hear

Will this replace our DAM? No. The goal is a practical editing and export layer for stills that complements your existing storage, chat, and review tools.

Mac only, or mobile too? We have not announced platforms yet. Tell us what you need in the early-access email so we can prioritize.

Will it read camera RAW stills? That is on the roadmap discussion list; mention your camera bodies in your email if RAW matters for your team.
