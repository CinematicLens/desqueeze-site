Moment Anamorphic Desqueeze Tool

Footage from Moment anamorphic lenses is usually 1.33× squeezed. Use our free Web Studio or apps to de-squeeze in one click: choose the 1.33× (Classic) preset, preview the correct CinemaScope framing, and export MP4, JPG or PNG. Works in the browser, on iPhone, Android, and Windows.

How to desqueeze Moment anamorphic footage

  1. Open the Web Studio or the iOS/Android app.
  2. Upload your clip or photo (batch up to 10 in the browser).
  3. Select 1.33× (Classic) in the de-squeeze preset.
  4. Check the preview, then export. No signup required.
