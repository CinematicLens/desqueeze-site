On-set or post: four steps

  1. Open Web Studio or the native app you already use in the field.
  2. Upload clip or still (batch up to 10 in the browser).
  3. Select 1.33× (Classic) and confirm framing in preview.
  4. Export — no signup, no project file.

Why this page exists

Moment footage is searched constantly with “how do I desqueeze 1.33×”. This is the straight path: correct ratio first, then move into edit or dailies. For other squeeze factors or brands, see Sirui or the full Web Studio guide.
